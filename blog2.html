Breaking Down The Alkaline Diet

The alkaline diet is also known as the acid-alkaline diet or alkaline ash diet. It is rooted in the idea that the metabolism's functions leave behind waste, which, depending where it falls on the pH range, is either acidic or alkaline. Acidic residue in the body is thought to make one vulnerable to illness, while alkaline residue is thought to be protective. By choosing more alkaline foods, you should be able to "alkalize" your body and improve health.
